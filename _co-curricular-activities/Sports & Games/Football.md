<h2>Football</h2>
<p>Saints Football is built on a tradition of passion, discipline, and teamwork.
More than just a sport, football at St. Andrew’s is a journey of character
development, resilience, and camaraderie. Our players embody the Saints
spirit—playing with heart, integrity, and determination.</p>
<h3>CCA Schedule</h3>
<p>Training Day(s): &nbsp; &nbsp;Tuesday and Thursday</p>
<p>Training Time: &nbsp; &nbsp;3.30 - 5.30 PM</p>
<p>Training Venue: &nbsp; &nbsp;SAS(S) Field</p>
<h3>Training Programme</h3>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Our structured training programme is designed to develop technical skills,
tactical awareness, and physical fitness while fostering a strong team
ethos.&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Technical Mastery – Ball control, dribbling, passing accuracy, shooting,
and first touch&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Tactical Awareness – Game intelligence, positional play, decision-making,
and team coordination&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Physical Conditioning – Speed, endurance, strength training, and injury
prevention&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Mental Toughness – Focus, resilience, leadership, and sportsmanship Our
dedicated coaching team ensures that every Saints footballer grows in skill
and character, preparing them not only for competition but for life beyond
the pitch. Whether representing the school in competitions or playing for
the love of the game, Saints Footballers strive for excellence in all they
do.</p>
</li>
</ul>
