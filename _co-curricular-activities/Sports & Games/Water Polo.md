<h2>Water Polo</h2>
<p>Water polo is one of the oldest team sports that was introduced Olympics
and over time has evolved into the toughest sport in the world. As a sport,
water polo requires its players to possess excellent endurance, strength
and agility to excel at this fast-paced and exciting sport.</p>
<p>Excelling at the National School Games level, our Saints water polo players
have gone on to represent the nation at various international stages, showcasing
their skill, determination, and sportsmanship. Their journey from school
competitions to national and global arenas reflects the rigorous training,
teamwork, and resilience that define the sport. Join Saints Polo today
to be a part of this legacy of excellence!</p>
