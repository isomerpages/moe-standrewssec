<h2>Rugby</h2>
<p>Since the introduction of rugby to St Andrew’s School in 1933, the school
has built a rich tradition of excellence in the sport. The school's dedication
to rugby has yielded impressive results, with the team consistently performing
well and earning top honours, including multiple championship titles. Additionally,
the school’s rugby program has been instrumental in nurturing boys on and
off the field. Many of these boys have gone on to represent Singapore at
various levels and this can be attributed to the school’s commitment to
helping every boy be their best as an athlete and a gentleman. True to
the spirit of ‘one family unbroken”, the tradition of alumni involvement
and parent support has been crucial in maintaining the team’s high standards
and passing down the rich rugby heritage to new generations of players.</p>
<p></p>
<p>“Hearts courageous scorn defeat;</p>
<p>So we press with eager feet,</p>
<p>Up and On!”</p>
