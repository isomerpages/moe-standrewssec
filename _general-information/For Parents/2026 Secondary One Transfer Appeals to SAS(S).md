<p>Students who wish to appeal to St Andrew’s School (Secondary) for admission
to Secondary 1 in 2026 can submit their appeals online via this link:
<a href="https://form.gov.sg/69296004d2f8c8642f00b239" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">S1 Appeal for Admission to St Andrew's Sch (Sec)_2026 | FormSG</a>
</p>
<p>The deadline for submission of appeal is 24 December 2025, 12.00 p.m.</p>
<p>Only students whose PSLE score meets our school’s cut-off point will be
considered, and acceptance is subject to available vacancies. The table
